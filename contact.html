Our Contact Process

The Springs Mold Solutions "Contact" process is meticulously designed for maximum effectiveness and minimal disruption to your Colorado Springs property. We follow a strict protocol established by industry best practices to ensure every step of the mold remediation and removal is handled with professionalism and precision. Our certified technicians are equipped with advanced tools and knowledge to manage any scale of mold infestation, prioritizing your safety and the long-term health of your environment. We communicate clearly at every stage, ensuring you're informed and comfortable with the work being performed, from initial assessment to final clearance. This systematic approach guarantees a thorough and lasting solution to your mold problems.

  1. Initial Assessment & Containment: Our team begins with a detailed inspection to identify the mold's extent and source, then sets up containment barriers using plastic sheeting and negative air pressure to prevent spores from spreading to unaffected areas of your Colorado Springs property. This crucial first step isolates the problem, protecting the rest of your home or business.
  2. Air Filtration & Mold Removal: High-efficiency particulate air (HEPA) filters are used to scrub the air of airborne mold spores while technicians carefully remove all mold-contaminated materials, such as drywall, flooring, or insulation, following strict safety protocols.   3. Cleaning & Drying: After physical mold removal, all affected surfaces are thoroughly cleaned and disinfected to eliminate any residual mold particles. Industrial-strength dehumidifiers and air movers are then deployed to dry out the affected areas completely, preventing any potential for future mold growth due to lingering moisture.

Restoration & Post-Remediation Verification: Once the area is completely dry and deemed mold-free, we proceed with necessary repairs and restoration to return your property to its original condition.
