Frequently Asked Questions

How much does mold remediation cost?

Minor localized issues range from $500–$1,500. Larger infestations with structural involvement typically run $3,000–$10,000. We provide a transparent, itemized estimate after the free inspection — no guessing.

How long does the process take?

Small, contained mold problems are resolved in 1–3 days. Larger infestations requiring extensive remediation and drying take 3–7 days. We'll give you a clear timeline upfront.

Do I need to leave during remediation?

For most projects, residents can remain in unaffected areas. For severe infestations, temporary relocation may be recommended for 24–48 hours. We'll advise you during the assessment.
