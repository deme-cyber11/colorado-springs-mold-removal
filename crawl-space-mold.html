Crawl Space Mold Removal in Colorado Springs

Complete crawl space remediation — vapor barriers, encapsulation, and mold-free guarantees.

Colorado Springs Crawl Space Mold Experts

Crawl spaces are ground zero for mold in Colorado Springs homes. The combination of ground moisture, poor ventilation, and temperature swings beneath the Pikes Peak region creates ideal conditions for mold colonies to thrive unseen — sometimes for years before homeowners notice the musty smell upstairs.

Our crawl space remediation goes beyond surface cleaning. We identify the moisture source, remove all mold growth, and install long-term moisture control systems so the problem doesn't come back.

Signs You Have Crawl Space Mold

  • Musty or earthy odor on the first floor of your home
  • Visible mold on floor joists, subfloor, or insulation
  • Standing water or damp soil in the crawl space
  • Sagging or soft spots in your flooring
  • Increased allergy symptoms indoors
  • Higher-than-normal humidity readings (above 60%)
  • Condensation on crawl space surfaces or ductwork

Our Crawl Space Remediation Process

1. Assessment: We inspect the entire crawl space for mold, moisture intrusion points, drainage issues, and structural concerns. Moisture readings are taken at multiple points.

2. Containment: The crawl space is sealed from the living area above using negative air pressure and HEPA filtration to prevent spore migration.

3. Mold Removal: All mold is removed from joists, subfloor, and structural members using HEPA vacuuming, wire brushing, and antimicrobial treatment. Contaminated insulation is removed and disposed of.

4. Moisture Control: This is the critical step. We install 20-mil vapor barriers across the ground, seal foundation vents, insulate rim joists, and install commercial dehumidification as needed.

5. Encapsulation (Optional): For full protection, we offer complete crawl space encapsulation — sealing the entire space with heavy-duty vapor barriers on walls and floor, plus a dedicated dehumidifier.

Why Crawl Spaces in Colorado Springs Are Vulnerable

Despite our semi-arid climate, Colorado Springs crawl spaces face unique moisture challenges. Spring snowmelt saturates the soil around foundations. Summer monsoons bring sudden ground moisture. And our dramatic temperature swings — 40°F differences in a single day — cause condensation on cold surfaces in crawl spaces.

Homes in Briargate, Security-Widefield, and Falcon are particularly prone due to the clay soil composition that holds moisture against foundations. Older homes in Old Colorado City and Manitou Springs often have inadequate vapor barriers or none at all.

⚠️ Never enter a crawl space with visible mold without proper PPE. Confined spaces concentrate spore levels to dangerous concentrations. Let professionals handle it.

Crawl Space Mold Removal Costs

Typical crawl space mold remediation in Colorado Springs runs $1,500–$5,000 depending on the size and severity. Full encapsulation adds $3,000–$8,000. We provide detailed written estimates after inspection — no surprises.
