Commercial Mold Removal in Colorado Springs

Mold remediation for offices, retail spaces, warehouses, and multi-unit properties — minimal disruption to your business.

Mold Remediation for Colorado Springs Businesses

Mold in a commercial property is more than a maintenance issue — it's a liability. Employee health complaints, tenant disputes, regulatory violations, and property damage can all escalate quickly when mold goes unaddressed in a commercial setting.

We specialize in commercial mold remediation that gets your space safe and operational with minimal downtime. Our team works around your schedule — nights, weekends, and phased approaches that keep portions of your building operational during remediation.

  • Schools, churches, and community buildings
  • Medical offices and healthcare facilities

Our Commercial Process

Initial Assessment: We conduct a thorough inspection and develop a remediation plan that accounts for your operations, occupancy, and timeline. Air quality testing establishes baseline readings.

Containment Strategy: Commercial spaces require more sophisticated containment — we use multi-chamber decontamination units, HEPA-filtered negative air machines, and polyethylene barriers to isolate affected areas while the rest of your building stays operational.

Remediation: All mold is removed following IICRC S520 standards. HVAC systems are inspected, cleaned, or isolated. Affected materials are removed and disposed of according to local regulations.

Clearance Testing: Independent post-remediation air quality testing confirms spore counts are within normal ranges. You receive written clearance documentation for your records, insurance, and regulatory compliance.

Why Commercial Mold Is Different

Commercial mold remediation is more complex than residential work. HVAC systems can spread spores across an entire building. Multiple tenants may be affected. ADA compliance, fire codes, and occupancy regulations add constraints. And the financial pressure of business interruption means speed matters without cutting corners.

In Colorado Springs, commercial properties along Tejon Street, in the Briargate business district, and near the airport corridor are common targets for mold due to older HVAC systems and flat commercial roof designs that trap moisture.

Insurance & Documentation

We work directly with commercial insurance adjusters and provide the documentation needed for claims: pre- and post-remediation air quality testing, itemized scope of work, photo documentation, and clearance certificates. Our detailed reporting helps expedite your claim process.
