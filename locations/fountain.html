Fountain, CO Mold Removal Experts

Fountain has been one of the fastest-growing communities in the Pikes Peak region, with new subdivisions expanding south along I-25 and east toward Pueblo County. While newer homes generally have better moisture barriers than older construction, Fountain's location along Fountain Creek — combined with the area's expansive clay soil — creates mold risks that affect homes of all ages.

We've handled mold jobs throughout Fountain, from the established neighborhoods near downtown to newer communities like Mesa Ridge, Cross Creek, and the homes surrounding Fort Carson's Gate 20.

Why Fountain Homes Get Mold

  • Fountain Creek floodplain — Properties near the creek corridor are subject to periodic flooding and elevated groundwater
  • Expansive clay soil — Swelling and contracting soil puts stress on foundations, creating cracks where moisture enters
  • New construction shortcuts — Rapid development sometimes means insufficient grading, rushed landscaping, and inadequate drainage
  • Basement condensation — The temperature difference between cool basements and warm summer air creates condensation that feeds mold

Services for Fountain Residents

  • Free mold inspection — comprehensive property assessment
  • Mold remediation — IICRC-standard removal and cleanup
  • Crawl space remediation — vapor barriers and moisture control
  • Black mold removal — safe handling of toxic mold species
  • Commercial mold services — for Fountain businesses
