Briargate Mold Removal Services

Briargate is one of north Colorado Springs' most popular residential communities, with homes ranging from 1990s builds in the established neighborhoods near Chapel Hills Mall to newer construction in Cordera and Pine Creek. While these homes are well-built, the clay-heavy soil common throughout Briargate holds moisture against foundations — creating conditions for crawl space and basement mold that many homeowners don't discover until it's well established.

We've helped dozens of Briargate families deal with mold issues, from minor bathroom mold to full crawl space remediation. Our team knows the common problem areas in Briargate homes and can often spot potential issues before they become expensive problems.

Common Mold Issues in Briargate

  • Basement mold — Many Briargate homes have finished basements where moisture intrusion goes unnoticed behind drywall
  • Crawl space growth — Clay soil retains snowmelt moisture against foundations through spring
  • HVAC mold — Older systems in 90s-era homes can harbor mold in ductwork and drain pans
  • Window condensation mold — Colorado Springs' temperature swings cause condensation that feeds mold around window frames
