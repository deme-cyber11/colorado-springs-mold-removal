Security-Widefield Mold Services

Security-Widefield is a close-knit community south of Colorado Springs, home to many military families from nearby Fort Carson and Peterson Space Force Base. The area's housing stock — a mix of 1960s–80s ranch homes and newer military housing — presents distinct mold challenges that we've become very familiar with over the years.

The flat terrain south of the city, combined with Fountain Creek's floodplain and the heavy clay soil, means that water drainage issues are common. When a home sits on poorly draining soil and has an aging foundation, crawl space moisture and basement seepage follow — and mold is close behind.

Common Issues in Security-Widefield

  • Slab-on-grade moisture — Many homes lack crawl spaces, but moisture wicks up through concrete slabs, causing mold under carpet and behind baseboards
  • Rental property neglect — High turnover in military rental homes means small leaks go unreported, leading to significant hidden mold
  • Swamp cooler residue — Older homes with evaporative coolers introduce excess humidity that feeds mold growth
  • Poor grading — Many properties slope toward the foundation rather than away, channeling water where it shouldn't go

Military Family Friendly

We understand the urgency military families face — PCS orders don't wait for mold remediation, and housing inspections require clear documentation. We provide expedited service with the written clearance reports that military housing offices and landlords require. We also work directly with property management companies throughout Security-Widefield.
