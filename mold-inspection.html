Mold Inspection in Colorado Springs

Comprehensive mold testing and assessment — free visual inspections available.

Thorough Mold Inspection & Testing

Suspecting mold but not sure where it is — or how bad it is? Our certified inspectors perform a detailed visual assessment of your entire property, including areas where mold commonly hides: behind walls, inside HVAC systems, in attics, crawl spaces, and around plumbing fixtures.

What's Included in Our Inspection

  • Complete visual inspection of all accessible areas
  • Moisture mapping with thermal imaging and pin meters
  • Identification of moisture sources (leaks, condensation, poor drainage)
  • Air quality sampling (optional, lab-analyzed)
  • Surface swab testing for mold species identification
  • Detailed written report with findings and recommendations
  • Remediation scope of work and cost estimate

Why Inspection Matters

Mold isn't always visible. It can grow inside wall cavities, beneath flooring, in HVAC ductwork, and in crawl spaces you never enter. A professional inspection identifies hidden mold, determines the species present, and pinpoints the moisture source that's feeding it.

In Colorado Springs, our dry climate can mask moisture problems. Homes here often have condensation issues from rapid temperature swings, and crawl space moisture from snowmelt that many homeowners don't realize exists until significant mold growth has occurred.

⚠️ Don't disturb suspected mold. Touching or scrubbing visible mold can release millions of spores into the air. Let a professional assess it first.

Free vs. Lab-Tested Inspections

Our free visual inspection identifies visible mold and moisture problems. If you need species identification or documentation for insurance/legal purposes, we offer lab-grade air quality testing ($200–$600) processed by an independent, accredited laboratory.
