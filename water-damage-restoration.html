Water Damage Restoration Colorado Springs

24/7 emergency water extraction, drying, and mold prevention for Colorado Springs properties.

⏰ Time is critical. Mold can begin growing within 24–48 hours of water exposure. Fast water damage response prevents costly mold remediation later.

Fast Water Damage Response

Whether it's a burst pipe, roof leak, appliance overflow, or storm flooding, water damage needs immediate attention. Our team responds 24/7 with commercial-grade extraction and drying equipment to minimize damage and prevent mold growth.

Our Water Damage Process

  • Emergency response — On-site within 2–4 hours in the Colorado Springs metro
  • Water extraction — Truck-mounted and portable extractors remove standing water fast
  • Moisture mapping — Infrared cameras and moisture meters identify all affected areas
  • Structural drying — Commercial dehumidifiers and air movers bring materials to safe moisture levels
  • Antimicrobial treatment — Prevention spray applied to stop mold before it starts
  • Monitoring — Daily moisture readings ensure proper drying progress
  • Documentation — Complete photo documentation for insurance claims

Common Causes in Colorado Springs

Colorado Springs homes face unique water damage risks: frozen pipes during harsh winters, afternoon hailstorms damaging roofs, rapid snowmelt overwhelming drainage systems, and slab leaks common in our clay-heavy soil. We've handled every type of water damage scenario in this area.

Pricing

Water damage restoration typically costs $3–$12 per square foot depending on the water category (clean, gray, or black water) and extent of damage. Emergency after-hours rates may apply. We work directly with your insurance company. See our cost guide for more details.
