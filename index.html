How It Works

Our Mold Removal Process

We follow a proven 4-step process that meets or exceeds all IICRC and EPA guidelines for safe, effective mold remediation.

1

Free Inspection

We perform a thorough visual assessment of your property to identify mold, moisture sources, and affected areas.

2

Containment

Negative air pressure and physical barriers prevent mold spores from spreading to unaffected areas during removal.

3

Remediation

HEPA-filtered air scrubbers and EPA-approved antimicrobials eliminate mold at the source, including inside walls and ductwork.

4

Verification

Post-remediation air quality testing confirms your property is safe. We provide a clearance report for your records.
