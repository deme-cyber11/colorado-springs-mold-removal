Mold Remediation in Colorado Springs

Complete mold removal following IICRC S520 standards — from containment to clearance verification.

Professional Mold Remediation

Mold remediation goes far beyond wiping down surfaces. It's a systematic process of containment, air filtration, removal, treatment, and verification that returns your indoor environment to safe, normal mold levels. Our team follows every step of the IICRC S520 standard to ensure thorough, lasting results.

Our Remediation Process

  • Assessment — We map the full extent of contamination and develop a remediation plan.
  • Containment — Physical barriers and negative air pressure prevent spore migration.
  • Air Filtration — HEPA air scrubbers capture airborne mold spores throughout the work area.
  • Removal — Contaminated materials (drywall, insulation, carpet) are safely removed and disposed of.
  • Treatment — Remaining structural surfaces are treated with EPA-registered antimicrobial solutions.
  • Drying — Industrial dehumidifiers ensure affected areas reach safe moisture levels.
  • Verification — Post-remediation air quality testing by an independent lab confirms clearance.

Why IICRC Certification Matters

The IICRC (Institute of Inspection, Cleaning and Restoration Certification) sets the industry standard for mold remediation. Our certification means we've been trained in proper containment techniques, safe removal procedures, and effective decontamination methods. It also means insurance companies recognize our work as meeting professional standards.

What to Expect

Most residential remediation projects take 1–5 days. We provide a detailed scope of work and written estimate before we begin. During the project, we keep you informed of progress and any unexpected findings. After completion, you receive a clearance report documenting that mold levels have returned to normal.

Typical costs range from $10–$25 per square foot depending on the extent of contamination. See our complete pricing guide for more detail.
