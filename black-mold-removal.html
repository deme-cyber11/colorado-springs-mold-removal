Black Mold Removal in Colorado Springs

Safe, certified removal of toxic black mold from your home or business.

⚠️ Health Warning: If you see dark, slimy mold in your home, do not touch, scrub, or try to clean it yourself. Black mold releases dangerous mycotoxins that can cause serious health problems. Call a professional.

What Is Black Mold?

Black mold (Stachybotrys chartarum) is a toxigenic mold species that produces mycotoxins harmful to humans and animals. It typically appears as dark green or black patches with a slimy or wet texture, and is most commonly found in areas with chronic moisture — behind walls, under sinks, in basements, and around HVAC systems.

Health Risks of Black Mold Exposure

  • Chronic respiratory issues and difficulty breathing
  • Persistent coughing, sneezing, and sinus congestion
  • Headaches, fatigue, and brain fog
  • Skin rashes and eye irritation
  • Allergic reactions and worsened asthma
  • In severe cases: neurological symptoms, immune suppression

Our Black Mold Removal Process

Black mold requires enhanced safety protocols beyond standard mold remediation:

  • Full PPE — Tyvek suits, full-face respirators, and gloves for all technicians
  • Negative air containment — Sealed work area with HEPA-filtered negative air machines
  • Safe material removal — Contaminated materials double-bagged and disposed of per EPA guidelines
  • HEPA vacuuming — All surfaces HEPA vacuumed to capture residual spores
  • Antimicrobial treatment — EPA-registered biocides applied to all remaining surfaces
  • Clearance testing — Independent air quality verification before we consider the job complete

Cost of Black Mold Removal

Black mold removal typically costs $15–$30 per square foot in Colorado Springs due to the additional safety measures required. A small area might cost $1,000–$3,000, while extensive contamination can exceed $10,000. See our cost guide for more details.
