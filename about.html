About Springs Mold Solutions

A locally operated team dedicated to keeping Colorado Springs homes and businesses safe from mold.

Who We Are

We're a team of IICRC-certified mold remediation technicians based right here in Colorado Springs. We started this company because we saw too many families dealing with mold problems that weren't being handled properly — or were being charged way too much for basic work.

Every member of our crew lives in the Pikes Peak region. We understand the unique challenges Colorado's climate presents — the dry air that masks moisture problems, the rapid temperature swings that cause condensation, and the afternoon storms that can lead to water intrusion.

We're not a franchise. We're not a national call center. When you call us, you're talking to the people who will actually be in your home doing the work. That's how we think it should be.
